Expert Insights

Our medical team shares professional perspectives on PCOS management.

Dr. Rodriguez, Endocrinologist

"While lifestyle changes are foundational for PCOS management, we now have good evidence that a combination of diet, exercise, and targeted supplements (like inositol) can be as effective as medication for many women with insulin-resistant PCOS."

Nutritionist Amy Wong

"Many women with PCOS benefit from focusing on protein and fiber at each meal to balance blood sugar. Try to include a vegetable, protein source, and healthy fat in every meal."
